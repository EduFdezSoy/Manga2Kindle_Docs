# Frequently Asked Questions

# What can it do?

Manga2Kindle can send mangas from your phone to your kindle device.

# What can't it do?

Manga2Kindle will never download mangas for you. You'll need to download them from other apps. (Check out: Tachiyomi)

# I'm new to the app, how can I use it?

I suggest you to read the Getting Started Guide.

# I'm sending mangas but nothing's dowloading in my Kindle!

Check out the Allow Receiving mangas from the app guide, maybe you are missing something.

# My Kindle's email? What's that?

That's the mail your Kindle watch to receive new documents, you can find this email in your device, check this.
